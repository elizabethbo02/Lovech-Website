Best Times to Visit Lovech

February 19th - Commemorative Torchlight Procession

Every year on February 19th in Lovech, a commemorative torchlight procession is hosted in memory of Vasil Levski. The event is organized by the patriotic civic organization 'Lovchani.' Over three thousand people participate in the procession every year. 'The aim is to honor the memory of Vasil Levski in his revolutionary capital in a fitting manner,' say the organizers of the event, emphasizing that with each passing year, more young enthusiasts are eager to contribute to the development of this procession. Over a hundred volunteers occasionally participate in organizing and securing the event. This is a very beautiful and emotional evening event, which is worth visiting.

April - The Blooming Of Lilacs

In the month of April, "Stratesh" and "Hisarya" are painted in blue, purple and white. Lovech smells of spring and lilac. Strates Park, on the right bank of the river, is a great place to enjoy the lovely view. The park is also known as "Lilac Park", because of the many plants of this type. In the spring, thousands of people come here to admire the delicate colors and unique lilac aroma.

Flower Sunday

The spring holiday Tsvetnitsa, also known as Vrabnitsa, is traditional for the city of Lovech. Every year on the days of the church holidays Lazarov's Day and Palm Sunday, since the end of the nineteenth century, a fair of arts and crafts has been held in the city. During the holiday of the holiday, a Regional Assembly of folk creativity is also organized.

May 11th - Lovech holiday

The official holiday of the city of Lovech is May 11, when, according to the old style, it is the Day of the Slavic script and of the Holy Brothers Cyril and Methodius. Residents quickly perceive this date as an official holiday, and many guests and delegations come on this day to the town every year. A few days before the actual date of the holiday, celebrations begin with events and activities such as concerts, exhibitions and sports events, which continue until the evening of May 11. Among them are the traditional "Lilac Music Evenings" for the city.

The Lighting of the Christmas tree

The Lighting of the Christmas Tree in Lovech is a cherished tradition that marks the beginning of the festive season in the town. Each year, as winter descends upon Lovech, residents watch the magical moment when the town square comes alive with twinkling lights and the Christmas tree is illuminated.
